ai-visitor-widget 🤖

Demo page — the floating widget at the bottom right is the live visitor counter. It pings /api/track with your current UA classified as human or AI, then fetches aggregate counts from /api/stats/:site. Reload the page to watch it increment.

Try it: open this page in your browser → your visit is logged as human. Then open via curl or a Python requests snippet → tagged ai.

Included example:

curl -X POST https://widget.yourdomain.com/api/track \
  -H "Content-Type: application/json" \
  -d '{"site":"demo","visitorType":"","userAgent":"GPTBot/1.0","path":"/","referrer":""}'
